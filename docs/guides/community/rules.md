# Camp Rules

Even Camp Crystal Lake has rules. Break them and you won't need Jason to worry about.

## The rules

1. **No cheating or exploiting** - don't abuse glitches or use external tools
2. **Respect all players** - no harassment, hate speech, or excessive toxicity
3. **No spamming** - don't flood chat, abuse mentions, or advertise
4. **Follow staff instructions** - when a Camp Director or Head Counselor tells you to stop, stop
5. **Keep content appropriate** - no NSFW, no illegal material, nothing that breaks Discord's ToS
6. **Report issues properly** - `#bug-reports` for bugs, `#suggestions` for ideas. `#general` isn't your complaint box

## How we handle breaks

We use a three-strike system:

1. **Warning:** you get a heads-up. Fix it and we move on
2. **Timeout:** temporary mute or kick. Usually a few hours to a day
3. **Ban:** done. You're out

How fast we escalate depends on how bad it is. Cheating or harassment skips straight to step 3.

!!! warning "Bans apply to the game server too"

    A Discord ban isn't just a Discord ban. Bans carry over to the Camp Crystal Lake game server. If you're banned on Discord, you can't log in with that PSN account on the server either.

## Appeals

If you think a timeout or ban was a mistake, DM any staff member on [Discord](https://discord.gg/dn4pacGHe3). Keep it calm, explain what happened, we'll look at it. No fixed response time, but we don't sit on appeals.

## Questions?

Ask in `#general` or DM staff. That's it.
