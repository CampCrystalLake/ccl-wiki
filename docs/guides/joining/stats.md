# Stats & Progress

Pamela keeps tabs on everything that happens at camp. Here's what gets tracked and how to check your record.

## Fresh start

Old stats from the official servers didn't come across. Everyone started from zero when the camp opened. No legacy accounts, no imports.

## Your progress stays

Stats stick around as long as the server does. We don't wipe. Close the game, come back a month later, everything's where you left it.

## What gets tracked

The short version: pretty much everything.

- **Matches** as Jason and as a counselor
- **Kills, escapes, deaths**
- **XP and level** (no level cap -- some players are already well past the original 150)
- **Perks, tapes, challenges, badges**
- **Feats** (broken down below)

### Counselor feats

- **Repairs:** car, boat, phone grid
- **Escapes:** car (driver/passenger), boat (driver/passenger), police
- **Calls:** police called, Tommy called
- **Combat:** Jason killed, Jason's mask off, sweater stuns, counselors killed
- **Survival:** window jumps, suicides, deaths by Jason, deaths by counselors
- **Tommy:** matches played as Tommy

### Jason feats

- **Destruction:** doors broken, walls broken, car slams
- **Combat:** throwing knife hits

## Check your stats on Discord

Pamela lives on [Discord](https://discord.gg/dn4pacGHe3) and answers three commands. They all take a PSN ID, so you can look up yours or anyone else's (as long as they've played on the server).

- `/player <PSN>` - overview: level, XP, matches, kills, escapes, tapes
- `/feats <PSN>` - the full feat breakdown above, split by counselor and Jason
- `/status` - live server health, online players, last heartbeat

No need to turn on your console to check.

## Your data is yours

Want your data exported, or want to move it to another server? DM staff on [Discord](https://discord.gg/dn4pacGHe3). We'll get it to you. If the other server's owner agrees to import it, it's yours to take.

## DLC items

If you own the DLC, it should be accessible. A few edge cases are still being verified. If something's missing, report it in `#bug-reports`.

Mother always keeps track.
